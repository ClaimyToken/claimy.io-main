BLACKJACK

How this round works

  • Each hand uses one fresh 52-card deck (shuffled once per game).
  • Dealer stands on 17+. Natural blackjack pays 3:2 on your main stake.
  • When the dealer shows an Ace, you may take insurance (half your main bet).
  • Double down doubles your main bet and draws exactly one more card.

Blackjack

Rules & provably fair

One standard deck per hand, shuffled with a deterministic Fisher–Yates using HMAC-SHA-256 counters claimy-bj|v1|<clientSeed>|<n> on the server secret (same pattern as Flowerpoker).

Gameplay

  • Number cards count as face value; J, Q, K count as 10; Aces are 1 or 11 (best total ≤ 21).
  • Dealer peeks for blackjack when showing an Ace or a ten-value card.
  • Insurance is offered only against a dealer Ace; it pays 2:1 if the dealer has blackjack.
  • Double down adds a second main bet and draws one card only.
  • Dealer draws until reaching 17 or higher, then stands.

Payouts

  • Win — 1:1 on the total amount you have on the hand (main + double).
  • Natural blackjack — 3:2 on the original main stake (before double).
  • Push — your main + double stakes are returned; ties on both blackjacks push the main hand.
  • Insurance — side bet up to half your main stake; pays 2:1 when dealer has blackjack.

Verification

After settlement, the server seed is revealed. You can confirm SHA-256(serverSeed) equals the committed hash, then re-run the shuffle with the same client seed to reproduce the deck order dealt to you and the dealer.
