Phantom registration

Three steps: pick a username and confirm it’s free, connect Phantom and sign one message, then complete registration. Your account is the link between that username and your public wallet—no password.

Security

We never ask for your seed phrase or private key. Phantom signs messages locally on your device; we only verify the signature on our server to prove you control the wallet you connect.
