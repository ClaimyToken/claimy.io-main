How this game works

  • One roll per bet: integer in 0–999 (1000 outcomes) from provably fair HMAC claimy-dice|v2|…|0.
  • Roll under — you win if the roll is strictly less than your target.
  • Roll over — you win if the roll is strictly greater than your target.
  • Multiplier ≈ (1000 ÷ winning outcomes) × 0.99 (1% house edge), capped at 500×; playable odds window is about 2%–98% win chance.

Credits lock and settle the same way as Flowerpoker and Blackjack (Edge + claimy_game_sessions).

Dice

Rules & fairness

Each bet samples one uniform index in 0…999 (1000 outcomes) using HMAC-SHA256 with label claimy-dice|v2|<clientSeed>|0 on the server secret. The committed hash is SHA-256(secret) before you roll.

Payouts

  • Hit — you receive stake × multiplier in credits (multiplier shown in the odds panel).
  • Miss — locked stake is lost for that round.
  • Boundary rolls (equal to the target) are losses for both under and over.

Verification

After settlement, reproduce roll = HMAC_uint32 % 1000 (v2 label), check the digest SHA-256(roll|mode|target|clientSeed), and confirm the hash of the revealed seed.
