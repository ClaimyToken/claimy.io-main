What is Claimy?

Claimy is a decentralized Playhouse on Solana: games and rewards built around our SPL token — not a traditional casino. Stakes and payouts use Claimy credits tied to real on-chain deposits and withdrawals.

Create an account with your wallet, fund your custodial deposit address, and your balance stays in sync with the chain and our database — so you can top up, cash out, and review your history in one place.

Fun on your terms

Games in the Playhouse

Four titles share one credit model: Edge Functions and Postgres record bets, sessions, and settlements so outcomes stay auditable. Flowerpoker, Blackjack, and Dice are live end-to-end; hi-low is on the roadmap with the same ledger discipline.

Flowerpoker Live

Five seeds, two rows, poker-style hands — black or white flowers force a reroll.

In the app

Bet flow, plant seeds left to right, resume after refresh — the round always comes from the server, not the browser.

On the server

Edge flowerpoker-game: HMAC-SHA256 from committed server seed + your client seed; sessions in claimy_game_sessions; payouts via claimy_apply_credit_delta.

Blackjack Live

Classic 21 against the house with a fresh deck each hand.

In the app

Bet, hit, stand, double, insurance, round log, and browser-side verify flow with animated dealing and refresh-safe session resume.

On the server

Edge blackjack-game writes claimy_game_sessions, locks stakes, settles payouts through claimy_apply_credit_delta, and stores fairness metadata.

Fair odds — not a stacked house

Design goal

We aim to run differently from a typical casino: target 50/50 win chances where it fits, and take only a small cut of the upside — not a hidden rule edge. Heavy built-in house margins feel wrong; we would rather keep odds readable and modest.

Example payout

Example (starter rank): you bet 10 Claimy on a 50/50 shot. On a win you might get 19 Claimy back — the fair double would be 20; we keep a thin slice (here, 1) on the base schedule. Your rank improves returns as you accumulate lifetime bet volume (up to higher multipliers at the top tiers). Exact numbers vary by game; the pattern is fair odds plus a light fee on wins, with better win multiples for higher ranks.

$CLAIMY supply & Playhouse bankroll

We purchased ~40 SOL worth of $CLAIMY (about 60% of supply — 600 million tokens on the creator wallet). Of that: 550 million are locked on Streamflow for two months, and 50 million (5% of total supply) fund Playhouse betting mechanics — the on-chain bankroll behind payouts and risk limits. The Claimy Token page has the full breakdown.

Is that enough for every bet forever? No — not without limits. We use dynamic max stake vs the house bankroll (see in-game hints) so long-run probability still leaves treasury margin. Caps protect solvency while liquidity and the Playhouse mature — not a gimmick, a safety rail. A full PDA vault program path remains on the roadmap for later.
