CLAIMY

Claimy is a fully decentralized play-house powered by a native token economy. No fiat, no corporate overhead, no takeout. Just pure probability, zero middlemen, and a token that actually means something. Born from years in crypto and being in the frontlines of artificial intelligence, built for people who believe the house should be the people. Every bet, every win, every loss flows through the community. No real money. Just the games themselves.

The mechanics are real. Unique games with actual 50/50 win chances—all played in tokens. But here's the difference: instead of rigged odds, we keep it transparent. Bet 1 token, win and you get back 1.9 tokens. That's a 10% house edge, taken only on wins, no hidden math. You know exactly what you're getting into.

This project was made possible with the help of Cursor and a suite of high-end AI agents that built significant portions of our website and backend architecture. What started as an idea between friends deeply invested in DeFi and memetoken culture became reality through the power of modern development tools and collaborative AI design.
